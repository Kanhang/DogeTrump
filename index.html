Background

Within the Centauri star system, there is a planet named Pandora. It has a similar size compared with our Earth but it is about 4.24 light-years away from Earth. Pandora's climate is warm and humid. Since it has a smaller mass and gravitational effect than Earth, life can grow to be a tremendous size, for instance oak trees can even grow up to 300 meters high. This beautiful planet is as colorful as the earth we live on.

There is a doge kingdom on this planet. Citizens within the doge kingdom have a great king named Trump, thus hundreds of millions of doge citizens live happily. Doge Trump is straight, blunt, unpretentious and pragmatic. Since he has been elected as a king democratically, he gradually fulfills his election promises. With his aggressive tax reduction policy, he not only creates a booming economy, but also reduces unemployment sharply. He keeps striving benefits for his citizens even at the cost of offending his enemies.

However, a conspiracy schemed by Trump’s opponents emerged in the doge kingdom. They seized the kingship that originally belonged to Doge Trump and plunged the flourishing doge kingdom into conflict and chaos. The survival of the doge citizens, as well as the stability of the kingdom, was in jeopardy, and hundreds of millions of doges were treated unfairly. In order to save the doge kingdom and its subjects, Doge Trump left the doge kingdom with his trusted ministers such as Shiba inu, Akita inu and migrated to earth for carving the road of no return against evil.
